My Windows Phone recommended apps list (Spring 2016)

10 May 2016

Current as of May 2016 and listed in alphabetical order, standard apps like Amazon and eBay are not included. I have not differentiated between paid and free apps, you have to check the current price in the app store, as some apps have been free or on a sale when I acquired them.

My apps list for Windows Phone 10:

  • 1Password - THE password manager on WP (read-only), just to have all those passwords in my pocket
  • Baconit - A WP reddit client, perfect to kill some time when I´m in the bathroom…
  • Dropbox - Alternative cloud file service
  • Kicktipp - A client for kicktipp.de to take part in our soccer tipping community
  • kodi remote free - A remote control app for my Raspberry Pi powered Kodi MediaCenter
  • mehr-tanken - Up to date gas prices, quite handy to save a few bucks
  • Nextgen Reader - RSS reader with awesome feedly support
  • 53 days to go - Shows how many days until a set date
  • Office Lens - Scan documents/flipcharts/Whiteboards to PDF
  • Podcast Picker - The best Windows Phone podcatcher I´ve used to date
  • RemindMe ToDo - A simple ToDo list, because I don´t need as fancy a list as the likes of Wunderlist
  • Telegram Messenger - Secure and open source messenger
  • Threema - My main messenger app, secure, but closed source
  • Toshl Finance - Book keeping for the simple guys
  • TripAdvisor - To check out where to sleep and to eat
  • Wikipedia - Just knows it all…
