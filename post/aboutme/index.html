About me

20 April 2016

Hey there and thanks for reading my blog!

My name is Lars and I´m currently doing my MBA at the University of Münster.

On this blog I will mainly publish Sketchnotes I doodled when I was learning or studying or listening to a talk or working on a project. I hope that those sketchnotes can help you, either as inspiration, that everybody can sketch and facilitate visual thinking processes, or maybe there are some doodles covering a topic that is of interest to you. In either case, if there is something missing or unclear, just shoot me a message or leave a comment on the respective blog post.
